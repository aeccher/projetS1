Glosssaire

Feminism

Ideology by which gender equality is restored and guarantees the expansion of the status of women in society

Equality of opportunity

Vision of equality aims to ensure that individuals have the same opportunities regardless of their social or ethnic origin, gender, economic status of parents, place of birth, religious beliefs…

Gender equality (between men and women)

Lack of gender inequalities, that is, men and women

Being illiterate

It’s not knowing how to read or write. It’s an illiterate person.

Neologism

Any word newly created or recently borrowed from another language, or new meaning brought to a word or expression existing in that language.

Pamphlet

Short violent texts attacking organizations, famous personalities.

Taliban

A member of an armed fundamentalist Islamic movement in Afghanistan. The Taliban belong to the Muslim fundamentalist movement that emerged in Pakistan. This movement launched a war against the Islamic State of Afghanistan. The organization is recognized as a terrorist organization by many countries around the world, such as the European Union and the United States.
